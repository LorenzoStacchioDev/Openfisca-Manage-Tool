[b]Instructions[/b]:
    - Select a variable through:
        - The spinner
        - The search box (click enter to search)
    - Insert into the text input form its value
    - Click on "Add Variable"
    - If you want to delete an inserted variable, click on it!

[b]Rules[/b]:
    - You can't insert a blank variable from the spinner
    - You can't insert a blank variable value
